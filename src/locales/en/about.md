# About aktivplan

The **aktiv**plan project is a multi-year research and development initiative focused on promoting sustainable health behavior change, with a focus on facilitating physical activity through digitally supported interventions. Co-developed with patients, healthy individuals, healthcare professionals, and researchers, the project has resulted in the **aktiv**plan application, designed to *bridge the gap between professional guidance and everyday life*. Core features include:

- **Personalized Planning:** Adaptive training plans tailored to individual needs and lifestyles.  
- **Shared Decision-Making:** Actively involves users in care decisions to support motivation and adherence.  
- **Motivation & Support:** Progress tracking, goal setting, and practical health tips.  
- **Monitoring & Feedback:** Healthcare professionals can review activity logs, provide feedback, and adjust plans.
