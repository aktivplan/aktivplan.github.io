For different use cases, the **aktiv**plan application offers several variants:

- **Healthy lifestyle promotion:** Users receive a suggested training plan but remain free in how they follow it.  
- **Rehabilitation:** Users receive a training plan and are encouraged to adhere to it.
- **Heat-resilience strengthening:** Users are supported in carrying out their training plan in accordance with weather conditions.

Additionally, various modules can be optionally configured:

- **Social features:** Allow users to connect and interact.  
- **Predefined training plans:** Professionals can prepare plans that can be further individualized when applied.
- **Health-app integration:** Supports connection with Apple Health and Google Health Connect.

**aktiv**plan is available as a web and native mobile app for convenient access on any device.
