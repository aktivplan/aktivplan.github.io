**aktiv**plan strengthens Austria’s healthcare and digital innovation landscape by promoting heart-healthy activity in preventive and rehabilitative settings. Developed with Austrian SMEs, hospitals, and research institutions, it contributes to job creation, competence building, and the growth of the digital health ecosystem. Key benefits include:

- **Alignment with national strategy:** Supports [Austria’s eHealth strategy](https://www.sozialministerium.gv.at/dam/jcr:d83aba44-320a-4f60-877a-4a8ecc9aaf4c/240204-eHealth-EN_bf.pdf&ved=2ahUKEwiV-d_93uyQAxV4gP0HHTaRAngQFnoECBoQAQ&usg=AOvVaw3f1g_OZdYwcN0CkhgLcMz8) and emerging reimbursement frameworks.  
- **Economic potential:** Enables sustainable business models and international export opportunities.  
- **Health impact:** Improves disease management, reduces physical inactivity, and enhances quality of life.  
- **System-level value:** Helps lower healthcare costs, expands access to care, and reinforces Austria’s role in digital health innovation.
