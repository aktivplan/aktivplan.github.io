**aktiv**plan is evolving to offer both a tool to enable further research as well as possible future use outside of research / pilot deployments for enhancing preventive and rehabilitative support. Upcoming features include:

- **Device integration:** Automatic data collection via activity trackers and smartwatches.
- **AI-driven personalization:** Training plans tailored to individual needs.  
- **Medical compliance:** Possibility of medical device certification for specific components and use cases to ensure reliable and standards-compliant care.

Potential future application perspectives include:

- Licensing the application to **health and rehabilitation centers**.  
- **Partnering with insurers** and integrate into digital health platforms.  
- Offering the application **directly to patients**, supporting broad adoption and sustainable growth.
